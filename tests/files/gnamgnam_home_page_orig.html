We enable vendors to easily set up their mobile "store" so nearby customers can access the app and place a food/merchandise order. The vendor receives the order in real-time, prepares it and delivers it to the customer at their specified seat/room/address.

We want every merchant anywhere in the world to be able to put its menu in everybody's smartphone. We connect customers with vendors with a single click.
